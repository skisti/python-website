Trip introduction

Lakeside Camping at Bhandardara at the Pristine Water Reservoir and dam camping at Bhandardara. It is one of the camping spot in Maharashtra and best weekend Outing Place besides Igatpuri. The village is located in the Ahmednagar district of the state of Maharashtra, near about 185 kilometres (115 mi) from Mumbai. Also known for best trekking place near Mumbai, Maharashtra. and it’s best weekend getaways near mumbai.

Bhandardara sits by the Pravara River, and the natural environment and most importantly, waterfalls, mountains, Wilson dam, Arthur Lake and Randha falls are tourist attractions. Warm summer winds and dam camping at bhandardara is tremendous. Recently more tourist attractracted to bhandardara because of Fireflies festival at bhandardara (Kajwa Festival) in the Purushwadi area. Bhandardara is the perfect Camping spot near Mumbai, where you can enjoy quality time with your loved once or with your family and experience the nature’s bliss. Bhandardara camps provides you different packages for camping at bhandardara. Fireflies Festival at bhandardara is mostly enjoyable in night camping.

Most importantly there are a number of historical and natural places of interest besides that dam. From Bhandardara, visitors can climb up to see the Ratangad and Harishchandragad. Or, visitors can follow the trails that besides that Ajoba and Ghanchakkar peaks. The highest peak in Maharashtra, Mount Kalsubai is the best trekking place in Maharashtra, 1646 m, and the starting point for this trek is Bari Village, located 12 km away from Bhandardara. At the top, there is a small temple located at the summit, and because views of Sahyadri’s and Bhandardara. Bhandardara Camping informs you about the toughest trio-forts because of Sahyadri namely Alang Fort, Madangad fort and Kulang fort (therefore they also called A-M-K) located on the southern side while you camping at bhandardara.
