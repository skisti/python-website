Camping at Shirota Lake

Lonavala, Maharashtra, India

Camping at Shiorta Lake

Shirota Lake Camp is nestled at the foothills of Rajmachi Fort, near Lonavala-Khandala, Maharastra. Due to the dense forest the area near and around Rajmachi Fort, it is the best place to camp and enjoy the wild life. Shirota Lake is a spot where every nature lover would love to explore again and again. A lake view camping experience in the accompaniment of delicious dinner and fun will definitely uplift you, emotionally and physically. Also, a time-bound campfire at night would be arranged. Shirota Lake is ideal for groups and families looking for an offbeat camping spot around Lonavala.

Location

Shirota Lake is 18 kms drive from Lonavala. Shirota Lake Camping spot is totally an unexplored place. Road leading to Shirota Camping is completely off road and challenge to drive on. It is preferable to take your personal mode of transportation like your bike or a car, since there is no scope of public transportation.
For reaching Shirota lake campsite catch a train from Dadar or to any station where express trains are halted. It is preferable to catch a train by morning where you can reach Lonavala mostly by 2.00 p.m. Once you reach Lonavala we will be leaving for Shirota Lake Camping through a private car. It is a 2 hr journey where you can view the beautiful landscape of Lonavala, waterfalls and a mountain scenic view.
