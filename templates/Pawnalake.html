Lonavala, Maharashtra, India

Camping at Pawna Lake

Pawna Lake is one of the best camping sites. Camping intent is the best way to celebrate the weekend with friends, family and college groups.The sheer size of the lake along with the surroundings make for an awesome sight. It is a very clean lake and too vast. It has a scenic beauty around, people come here for camping and photography . The lake is one of the popular artificial lakes located 10 kilometres has become an important campsite after the construction of the Pawna Dam.

Location

Pawana Lake is about 20 kilometres from the Lonavala Railway Station.
To reach Pawna lake camp catch a train from Dadar or to any station where express trains are halted. It is preferable to catch a train by morning where you can reach Lonavala mostly by 2.00 p.m. Once you reach Lonavala we will be leaving for Pawna Lake Camping through a private car. It is a 2 hr journey where you can view the beautiful landscape of Lonavala, waterfalls and a mountain scenic view.
